About METY Technology

METY Technology, Inc. is a woman-owned, minority-owned company that specializes in developing AI and machine learning-based software that provides innovative solutions to major societal challenges.

Our Mission

METY Technology's mission is to develop technology to better the human condition.

Leadership Team


Yihong Sun

Chair and Chief Financial Officer

Ms. Sun is the Chair of METY's board and its chief financial officer. She has an MBA and over 20 years of professional experience. Her primary specialties are finance and accounting. Previously, she has founded and run her own profitable companies.

Dr. John Leddo

President

Dr. Leddo is President of METY Technology. He has founded and run companies for over 30 years. Moreover, he holds a PhD from Yale University and has published over 100 scientific papers, many in the field of medical machine learning.



Ken Kaplan

Chief Technology Officer

Mr. Kaplan is METY's Chief Technology Officer. He has over 30 years of experience in the field of technology, having held positions that have run the gamut from programmer to project and product manager to chief technology officer and chief operating officer. He previously founded a technology company that he grew to $30 million in annual revenue and subsequently sold.

Dr. Taruna Agarwal

Medical Director

Dr. Agarwal is METY's medical director. She received her medical degree in 1999 from Government Stanley Medical College and Hospital where she was trained in the field of nutrition. Dr. Agarwal leads METY's medical machine learning research program and has published several papers in that area.
